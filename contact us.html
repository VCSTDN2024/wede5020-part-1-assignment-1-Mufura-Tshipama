Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can place an order through our website order form or by contacting us on WhatsApp.

Do you deliver?

Yes! We offer delivery within our local area. Delivery fees may apply based on location.

Can I customize my cake?

Absolutely! Just select “Custom Design” on the order form and share your ideas with us.

How early should I order?

We recommend placing your order at least 3 days in advance for custom designs and large orders.
